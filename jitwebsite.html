ABOUT US

Jahangirabad Institute of Technology, Barabanki was founded in 2001 that is situated in the historic fort of Jahangirabad, in Barabanki District, about 40 km from Lucknow city. It is a private institute which is providing professional education in the field of Engineering and technology. JIT will provide facilities for rewarding educational experiences that develop the capabilities for independent thought, critical judgment, problem-solving, effective communication & ethical sensitivity, to equip graduates for leadership roles in business, industry, government setup, and society. Jahangirabad Institute of Technology, Barabanki is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. JIT is known as the top B.Tech college in Lucknow & Barabanki area and aims at making their engineering students industry-ready professionals so that they are able to be absorbed by the best organizations in their respective domains.
